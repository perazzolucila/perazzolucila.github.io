About

I’m passionate about language and communication in all its forms. Over the years, I've delved into these fields, studying translation, Spanish proofreading, UX writing, subtitling, and starting a degree in school librarianship. All this has given me a comprehensive understanding of how words are used from various perspectives. The services I offer include English to Latin American Spanish translation, proofreading, localization, MTPE, subtitling, and transcription.
